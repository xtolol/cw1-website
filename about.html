About Us.

"Promoting the growth and sustainability of small businesses in our community."

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading reservation app for local services, recognized for our commitment to exceptional customer experiences and sustainable community development. We strive to create a platform that enables people to easily discover and book the services they need while supporting local businesses and promoting responsible practices. We believe that by connecting people with the services they need, we can create stronger, more vibrant communities that benefit everyone.

Mission

Our mission is to provide convenient and reliable access to local services through our reservation app, while also promoting the growth and sustainability of small businesses in our community. We are committed to delivering exceptional customer service and fostering a sense of connection and support among our customers and partners. Our ultimate goal is to make it easy for people to find and book the services they need, while contributing to the vitality and prosperity of our local economy."

Meet the team

At Rezervet, we believe that our team is our greatest asset. Our leadership team brings decades of experience in business and technology, and is dedicated to driving innovation and excellence in everything we do. We also take great pride in our talented and diverse team of developers, designers, and customer service professionals, who are passionate about delivering the best possible experience for our users. Together, we strive to create a workplace culture that fosters collaboration, creativity, and personal growth, and empowers our team members to reach their full potential.
